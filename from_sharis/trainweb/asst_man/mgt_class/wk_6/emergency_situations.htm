EMERGENCY PROCEDURES

Have you ever been in an emergency situation and you just guessed that what you were doing was correct. How about at home. What do you do when the power goes out, a fire in the oven? We have all experienced the feeling of "What do I do?"

That is why the Emergency Situation Binder was created. Every possible situation is included in this binder. We ask managers to make sure that if they should experience a crisis situation that is not included to please let us know how they resolved the situation. We in turn will update the binder with a new Emergency Situation Sheet. This way the next manager to experience the same situation can look up the procedures in the binder.

   

Each situation is noted by a divider tab. Each tab includes an action plan of the steps to follow in case of an emergency and the contacts needed. Reopening procedures are addressed in tab 3 - Emergency Evacuation Procedures. Anytime you must close the restaurant as the result of an emergency or other situation, you should use the checklist to insure you have followed all the policies and procedures required.

   
Blank forms are provided in the last tab of the binder. These are the forms you would use if you experience an emergency situation that we do not have included in the book. After dealing with the situation, please fill out the form and pass it on to your DM or the training department.
