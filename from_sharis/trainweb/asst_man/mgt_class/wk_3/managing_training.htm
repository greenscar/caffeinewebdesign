Managing Training
   
 

Shari's has developed a program to manage training in the stores. It is called Shari's CEO Program. The acronym CEO stands for "Creating Excellence Officer". This program is a partnership between the store managers and a select group of employees that they choose to train their new employees. This partnership relies on both the management team and the trainers fulfilling their responsibilities and commitment to:

  • ensure that new employee receives excellent training.
  • support the training effort with time, talent and the tools needed to accomplish the task.
  • communicate openly to improve themselves and their program.
  • invest in their development and continue to grow in knowledge and skill.
  • Work together and support one another.

To learn about Shari's CEO program, gather your store's "Managers' Training Manual" and turn to the "CEO Training Program" tab. You are to read pages 1-20 (don't worry, it's not that bad). Log into the Chat Room by 3:45 for a discussion about your store's CEO program.
