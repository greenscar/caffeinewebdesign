Security and Banking Procedures

   
The following are Shari's policies regarding security and banking procedures.   These procedures have been adopted to secure the company's assets and insure our managers' safety.   Failure to follow these procedures can result in immediate dismissal.   Please read this form and then fax it to your Training Manager.
 

Banking:

  1. Deposits must be made before 11:00 a.m., Monday through Sunday.

  2. Night deposits are required on weekends.

  3. Night drop receipts are to be picked up on Mondays.   These should be verified to the daily sales log immediately after they are picked up.

  4. Double verify all deposits and change transactions with the bank.

  5. Keep tabs from night bags.

  6. If a supervisor goes to the bank, the next manager on duty verifies that the deposits were accurately done.

  7. If your store accepts checks, managers are to write the name, telephone number, check number, and amount of 1 personal check submitted with each day's deposit on the deposit slip.

 

Security - General :

  1. The safe remains locked – no day locks.

  2. The back door remains locked – use deadbolt.

  3. The office door is to be locked at all times while counting cash and whenever the safe is open.

  4. The office door remains locked – deadbolt only.

  5. Keys are distributed only to general and assistant managers.   Supervisors need to return keys on the next shift.

  6. Any change in management will result in an immediate change of office lock and safe combination.

  7. The office key will be in the on-duty manager's possession at all times.

  8. Safe logs are mandatory.   The safe is required to be audited with entries made in the safe log a minimum of 3 times daily or at all management shift changes.

  9. Cash pulls are mandatory:
    • Registers should be checked hourly,
    • $500 maximum in register while a manager is on duty
    • All $50 and $100 will be moved to the office safe when received
  10. When a manager leaves during graveyard there will be $250 maximum in register.
  11. The safe will always balance.   Change added to the register will be bought from the safe.
  12. No server tips will be cashed in at the cash register.
  13. Personal and payroll checks are not to be cashed at Shari's.   This also applies to Shari's employees, including managers.
  14. Company funds are not available for personal use.

Security Cameras:

  1. It is the manager's responsibility to insure that the video cameras are constantly recording. Make sure the red light above the "record" button is lit/on. 
  2. The District Manger must approve any adjustment in the direction of the video cameras.
  3. Any blocking or tampering with the video camera system will be investigated by the District Manager and will result in severe disciplinary action.
  4. Employees taking 10 minute breaks out back, must remain by the back door so that they can be within camera view.

                                                                                      

ATM Security:

  1. ATM day close is to be completed by 8:00am daily.
  2. The ATM will remain locked at all times - no day lock on the combination lock.
  3. Counting of the ATM cassette will be done in the office.
  4. ATM will be stocked and verified to the approved level and the "Quantity in cassette" will be entered into the ATM each morning.
  5. The ATM cash level will be checked between 4 and 5pm daily to avoid opening the machine during busy times.
  6. The ATM machine is to be stocked when the cash level falls below 50% of the approved cash amount - a "cash paid out" will be made from day shift and the "Quantity in cassette" amount must be increased by the restocking amount.
  7. Morning stocking cash will be taken from Shari's sales. If available cash is insufficient, then money will be taken from lottery revenue and the District Manager will be notified.

 

Lottery Security:

  1. Lottery cash box keys will remain in the safe and will not be given out to any unauthorized person.
  2. Video poker machines are to be emptied between 6am and 7am - the video poker area will be cleared when emptying machines in the morning.
  3. Cash in the video poker machines will be pulled between 3pm and 5pm when the payouts exceed $1500 or the play exceeds $4000. The money pulled from video machines will be verified to reports after pulls.
  4. The lottery bank will be balanced at 7am, 3pm, 11pm and 2:30am. The lottery bank log must be used when balancing the lottery bank.
  5. A manager will be involved in all lottery payouts that exceed $100.
  6. $500 maximum is to be kept in the lottery cash drawer. This amount may be decreased or increased depending on player frequency and District Manager approval.
