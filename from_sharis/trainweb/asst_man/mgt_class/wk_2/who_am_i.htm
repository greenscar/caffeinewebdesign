10 Questions/Who Am I
 
Your instructor will divide you up into teams and give you each a team name. Each team will be emailed a “bug”.   You will study your “bug” and then the other teams will take turns asking 10 questions to determine what “Bug” you have.
 
  • Total questions combined teams can ask is up to 10 questions. (For each bug).
  • Teams take turns in the chat room asking one question at a time.
  • At the end of a question, after the team answers, you may make a guess as to what “bug” they have – if you answer incorrectly, the next team gets a chance to guess.
  • When you guess correctly you are awarded points based on the number of unasked questions remaining. For example:
 
•  If 4 questions were asked before a correct guess was made, the team making the correct guess would be awarded 6 points. (10-4=6)
 

•  In the event that no team can figure out your “bug”, the team answering questions receives 10 points.
