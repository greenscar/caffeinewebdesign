Shari's Mission Statement

To enhance the value of our guests' overall dining experience by identifying and developing extremely talented and caring employees dedicated to serving quality products with pride.

 

One of the key words in Shari's mission statement is Identifying. According to the dictionary, identify means to - recognize or connect closely with. And just what are we trying to identify? Well, according to the mission it is talented and caring individuals. In essence, we are saying that we must have a talented and caring individual, everything else is trainable. This must be our first concern as interviewers. Over the next two days we will be learning about Shari's interviewing techniques and policies.
