Using the Chat room
   
   

Shari's Corporate University Chat Room function is to be used as an interactive learning tool. Chat Room participation is used in many online assignments to share ideas, learn from guest presenters and enhance your learning experience by simulating a live classroom environment. Following are the basic codes of conduct and instructions for using the chat Room.

Code of Conduct

Respect : Please show respect for one another at all times. You may disagree with a statement, but you must do so politely. No profanity or degrading remarks (such as, that idea is stupid) are allowed. Always use good common sense and don't speak to anyone in a manner that you wouldn't like to be spoken to.

Typing : Not everyone is a fast and accurate typist, and that is not the point of the chat room. Be thoughtful in your comments, but we will not be concerned with spelling, grammar or punctuation. The idea is to keep the conversation going. However, not everyone understands chat room. lingo, please type out all of your comments fully, don't use internet talk such as lol (laugh out loud).

Participate : Participate in the conversation; the sharing of ideas is the point of the assignment. There is no such thing as a dumb idea or question. Many chat room. assignments are graded for points. On the other hand, don't hog the conversation either.

Be on Time : Unlike the bulletin board the chat room. assignments are synchronized, which means that everyone is online at the same time. Be courteous to your instructor and fellow students by joining the chat room. at the assigned time.

To join an open chat room. either click on the link in the assignment or go to http://university.sharis.com/chat . Type in your name, using your real name please, no alias or name other than the one you go by. After you type in your name, click “ Login”

 
You are now in the lobby of the Chat Room. To enter a Chat Room, click on the "Rooms" button.
When you click on the "Rooms" button, you will see a list of available chat rooms. Select the chat room of your instructor by clicking on it and then click on the "Change room" button at the bottom.
You are now in the Chat Room for your class. Your screen will look like the one below.


Type your comments in the message box. To post your comment, click on "Send" or simply press enter on the keyboard. Your comments will post on the large board above. As your fellow students and instructors post replies, read their comments and respond to them yourself. Again, don't worry too much about spelling, grammar or punctuation. The important thing is to read the comments and respond thoughtfully.

Settings: There are a few things that we can do to help distinguish ourselves from each other and make the Chat conversation easier to follow.
Avatar Icon: Click on the "My Profile" button on the top menu bar and select on "avatar icon". An avatar is a small picture that will identify you along with your name in the chat room.

The above menu appears below the message box. Use this menu to select the following.

Color: Chat postings are easier to read when each person uses a different color. Please use the same color for both your name and text. Do not select Red or Blue, Red is reserved for the instructor and Blue is reserved for guest speakers.

Choose a color by clicking on the color button.

From the color chooser, click on the same color for your name and test. Close the window when you are done.
Images: Click on the "Image" button to include a small expressional picture with your posting. Please, do not overuse this feature.
When it is getting to the end of the Chat Session, the instructor may post some final instructions. To leave the Chat Room, click on the "logout" button on the bottom menu. Continue to follow the daily schedule and complete all assignments until class ends at 5pm.
